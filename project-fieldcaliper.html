Frog Twissell- Firmware design

Introduction

Prototype - reached low volume production. Retired post employment. Designed to measure shellfish and store the measurement via bluetooth to a cellphone app. Accuracy was around +/- 0.5mm which was sufficient for the application.

I produced many 3D printing experiments here, and a lot of CAD models. This was one of my first real design projects and in retrospect my time was too heavily weighted in CAD and 3D printing
